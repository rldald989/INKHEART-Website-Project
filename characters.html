Characters

Meggie


Meggie is the daughter of Mo(Mortimer) and seems to be the main character of INKHEART, and like her father, she has the power to red things out of books.



Mortimer


Mortimer, or Mo, is the father of Meggie and is one of the main protagonists, he has the power to red things out of books.



Dustfinger


Dustfinger is a master of fire, read out of the book INKHEART by Mo.(often referred to as the matchstick eater)



Elinor


Elinor is the great aunt of Meggie, she is also the most giant bookworm known to man(and woman).



Fenoglio


Fenoglio was the writer of INKHEART(the INKHEART in the story, not the real life one), and was the one to write the end to the story that ended Capricorn's reign terror and cruelty.



Farid


Farid is an aribic boy from the story Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves, he was also read out of his book by Mo, but at a much later time, and is apprentice to Dustfiger.



Capricorn


Capricorn is the main villain of INKHEART, both in the INKHEART in the story and out, he is a ruthless man with cold eyes and a dark determination, a pure menace.



Basta


Basta, master of knives was for a great time, Capricorn's right-hand man, but is superstitious and by the end of the story angry and confused.
