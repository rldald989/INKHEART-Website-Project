About

The book INKHEART was created by the German author Cornelia Funke and translated to English by Anthea Bell.

INKHEART is a part of a trilogy series written by Funke, including INKHEART, INKSPELL, and INKDEATH.

INKHEART was published in the year of 2003 on September 23rd of that year.

*The following information was resourced from the back of an INKHEART paperback copy*

INKHEART was a
"New York Times Bestseller",
"USA Today Bestseller",
"ALA Notable book",
"2003 Publishers Weekly Best Children's Book",
and "2003 Kirkus Reviews Editor's Choice".
